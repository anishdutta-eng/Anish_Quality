### 3. 🧠 Intelligent Health Scoring

**Your Feedback:** "Create a function that takes the last 10 iterations to judge the health. Main parameter to use is MCS, SNR, and NSS."

**Research Findings:**

Based on WiFi 6 (802.11ax) industry standards:

| Metric | Excellent | Good | Fair | Poor |
|--------|-----------|------|------|------|
| **SNR** | ≥35 dB | ≥25 dB | ≥20 dB | <20 dB |
| **MCS** | 10-11 (1024-QAM) | 8-9 (256-QAM) | 6-7 (64-QAM) | <6 |
| **NSS** | 4 streams | 3 streams | 2 streams | 1 stream |

**Sources:**
- [itnet.lu](https://itnet.lu/wifi-tools/): "For Wi-Fi 6, SNR ≥ 25 dB is recommended for higher MCS indices"
- [interline.pl](https://www.interline.pl/ru/node/194): "802.11ax APs need very high SNR thresholds (~35 dB) to use 1024-QAM modulation"

#### New Function: `evaluate_network_health_advanced()`

**Scoring System (100 points total):**

1. **MCS Index (40 points)**
   - MCS 10-11: 40 pts (1024-QAM - Outstanding)
   - MCS 8-9: 35 pts (256-QAM - Excellent)
   - MCS 6-7: 28 pts (64-QAM - Good)
   - MCS 4-5: 20 pts (Fair)
   - MCS 2-3: 10 pts (Poor)
   - MCS 0-1: 5 pts (Very Poor)

2. **SNR (35 points)**
   - SNR ≥35 dB: 35 pts (Supports 1024-QAM)
   - SNR ≥25 dB: 28 pts (Supports 256-QAM)
   - SNR ≥20 dB: 20 pts (Supports 64-QAM)
   - SNR ≥15 dB: 12 pts (Supports 16-QAM)
   - SNR <15 dB: 5 pts (Very Poor)

3. **NSS - Spatial Streams (15 points)**
   - 4 streams: 15 pts (4x4 MIMO - Excellent)
   - 3 streams: 12 pts (3x3 MIMO - Good)
   - 2 streams: 8 pts (2x2 MIMO - Fair)
   - 1 stream: 4 pts (1x1 SISO - Basic)

4. **Stability Bonus (10 points)**
   - MCS variance <1 & SNR variance <4: 10 pts (Very stable)
   - MCS variance <2 & SNR variance <9: 7 pts (Stable)
   - MCS variance <4 & SNR variance <16: 4 pts (Fair)
   - Higher variance: 0 pts (Unstable)

5. **MCS-SNR Consistency Check**
   - Penalty if SNR is too low for the MCS index
   - Ensures realistic health assessment

- 0-29: Critical

## Technical Details

### Error Suppression Methods

1. **Shell redirection:** `2>/dev/null`
   - Redirects stderr to null device
   - Prevents error messages from appearing

2. **Python subprocess:** `stderr=subprocess.DEVNULL`
   - Suppresses stderr at Python level
   - More robust than shell redirection

### Health Scoring Algorithm

**Step 1:** Collect last 10 iterations
```python
recent_iterations = csv_data[-10:]
```

**Step 2:** Extract and average metrics
```python
avg_mcs = sum(mcs_values) / len(mcs_values)
avg_snr = sum(snr_values) / len(snr_values)
avg_nss = sum(nss_values) / len(nss_values)
```

**Step 3:** Calculate variance (stability)
```python
mcs_variance = sum((x - avg_mcs) ** 2 for x in mcs_values) / len(mcs_values)
snr_variance = sum((x - avg_snr) ** 2 for x in snr_values) / len(snr_values)
```

**Step 4:** Score each component
- MCS: 40 points max
- SNR: 35 points max
- NSS: 15 points max
- Stability: 10 points max

**Step 5:** Apply consistency check
- Verify SNR is sufficient for MCS
- Penalize if mismatch detected

**Step 6:** Normalize and categorize
- Score: 0-100
- Status: Excellent/Good/Fair/Poor/Critical
