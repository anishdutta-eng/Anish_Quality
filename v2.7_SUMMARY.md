## Key Features

### Rate vs Range Plot
**Purpose:** Show how MCS and SNR degrade with distance

**Interpretation:**
- Close to AP (0-10m): High MCS (9-11), High SNR (30-40dB)
- Medium range (10-20m): Good MCS (6-8), Good SNR (22-30dB)
- Far from AP (20-30m): Low MCS (3-5), Fair SNR (15-22dB)
- Out of range (>30m): Very low MCS (0-2), Poor SNR (<15dB)

**Use Cases:**
- Optimize AP placement
- Validate coverage area
- Identify coverage gaps
- Determine AP spacing for mesh

### Distance Tracking
**Purpose:** Visualize movement patterns during test

**Benefits:**
- See how far you've walked
- Correlate distance with performance
- Understand mobility impact
- Validate coverage at specific distances
